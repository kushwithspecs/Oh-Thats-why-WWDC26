Physics has always been taught through diagrams and textbooks. but forces 
aren't meant to be read about — they're meant to be felt. oh, that's why!

---

## The Problem

I was 12 when I first learned about Newton's laws. I understood the 
equations. I passed the tests. But I never had that moment of 
*oh, that's why*. I never once felt a force. I only ever read about them.

That gap bothered me. So I built something to close it.

---

## What It Does

Oh, That's Why! uses the gyroscope, accelerometer, and gravity sensor 
already inside your device — combined with SwiftUI, SpriteKit, and 
CoreMotion — to turn physics from something you read into something 
you feel.

5 activities. No textbook. No prior knowledge needed.

| Activity | Concept |
|---|---|
| Balance the Ball | Equilibrium · Newton's First Law |
| G-Force Lab | Gravity · Vector Components |
| Physics Drop | Newton's Laws · Friction · Momentum |
| Pendulum Timer | Indirect Measurement · T = 2π√(L/g) |
| Predict & Move | Acceleration · Newton's Second Law |
